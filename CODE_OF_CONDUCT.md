# Code of conduct

This project follows the principles of the
[Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

Participants must discuss technical disagreements respectfully, give specific
and constructive feedback, respect personal boundaries, and protect sensitive
information disclosed through security or conduct reports. Harassment,
discrimination, threats, doxxing, unwelcome sexual attention, deliberate
intimidation, and sustained disruptive behavior are not accepted in project
spaces or project-related private communication.

Email `alex@alexdoes.it` with the subject `aiignore conduct` to report conduct
concerns privately. Do not disclose personal or sensitive information in a
public issue. Reports will be handled confidentially to the extent practical.
Maintainers may remove content, issue a private or public warning, restrict
participation, or permanently ban a person when needed to protect the
community. A maintainer named in a report will not be the sole decision-maker
when another qualified reviewer is available.
